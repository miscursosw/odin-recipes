Chicken Thighs

Description

Delicious Chicken Thighs for 4 people.Serve with basmati rice or quinoa and steamed or roasted vegetables.

Preparation time 10 minutes.

Cooking time 60 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil

Steps

  1. Lay chicken thighs into the bottom of a 4-quart slow cooker.
  2. Whisk soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, and basil together in a bowl; pour over the chicken.
  3. Cook on Low for 6 hours.
